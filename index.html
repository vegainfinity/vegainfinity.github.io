  • About Us

  • Vega Infinity was set up by four university students with first-hand experience of the issues faced when looking for insurance. Our platform allows you to easily search for, compare and buy travel and gadget insurance.
  • Filling in a form has never felt better; our easy-to- use interface speeds us the ‘getting to know you’ process. Provide us with details on the insurance you would like to take out, hit search and leave the rest to us.
  • We will provide you with a list of policies that best match your needs, cheapest policy first. If you want to know more about an insurance provider or rank by Defaqto Star Rating then you can do this too.
  • If you’re having trouble with insurance jargon then make use of our guides and FAQ page. For personal and impartial advice speak to one of our consultants by phone or via our online chat service.
